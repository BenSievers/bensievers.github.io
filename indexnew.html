I am currently an NIH Oxford-Cambridge PhD Scholar working between the Vaccine Research Center at the National Institutes of Health and the University of Cambridge.

My research focuses on vaccine immunity, antibody-mediated protection, viral evolution, and cancer immunotherapy.

Current projects include SARS-CoV-2 immunity in older adults, antibody effector functions following vaccination, and innate immune priming strategies to improve checkpoint blockade in cold tumors.
